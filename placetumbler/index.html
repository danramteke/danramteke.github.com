PlaceTumbler

Ever wanted to post a quite note about the delicious restaurant that you're eating at right now? Ever wanted to write a GPS-tagged review of a scenic overlook while hiking? PlaceTumbler can help.

PlaceTumbler posts to your Tumblr blog your restaurant reviews. It also tags them with your current latitude and longitude. All's you need is a free Tumblr account and a location aware iPhone or iPod Touch.

PlaceTumbler It's location for Tumblr. Now available on the App Store.
